# Code of Conduct

## Our Commitment

We are committed to providing a welcoming and inclusive environment for all contributors and users of the Oncology Dashboard project.

## Expected Behavior

- Be respectful and inclusive
- Provide constructive feedback
- Focus on what is best for the project
- Show empathy towards others
- Accept responsibility and apologize when appropriate

## Unacceptable Behavior

- Harassment, discrimination, or intimidation
- Offensive language or comments
- Unwelcome sexual attention or advances
- Trolling, spamming, or deliberate disruption
- Doxxing or publishing private information without consent

## Enforcement

Instances of unacceptable behavior should be reported confidentially. All complaints will be reviewed and investigated.

This Code of Conduct is adapted from the Contributor Covenant.
